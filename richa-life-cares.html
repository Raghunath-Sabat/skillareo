Challenges, Results, Growth & Success Story - Richa Life Cares

About Richa Life Cares

Richa Life Care is all about daily lifestyle vlogs, travel vlogs, haircare, skincare, makeup, beauty, style, fitness. She loves creating vlogs, traveling to different places and covering them on camera, meeting new people, and sharing her knowledge with her fans.

Goal

Before the intervention of Skillareo, Richa Life Cares had published so many videos. The videos were very entertaining and of immense value to the audience. They did this consistently as experts had recommended. They also shared several short videos on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. They were working really hard, doing everything accordingly. However, the growth was not good enough. It was not difficult to tell something was missing. Our goal was to Plan and create a profitable YouTube marketing campaign to boost youtube video views and Increase social media fan following and loyalty.

Challenges

  1. Planning a YouTube marketing strategy to give visibility to Richa's channel, personal image, and at the same time, creating content that both engages and entertains their viewers in order to build a community.
  2. Youtube market research.
  3. Increase social media fan following and loyalty.

Services Provided By Skillareo

  • Youtube Marketing & Management.
  • Facebook Marketing, Management & Advertising.
  • Instagram Marketing, Management & Advertising

Solutions

  1. Creating an effective Youtube, Instagram & Facebook marketing strategy.
  2. Analysing competitions and learning from the best YouTubers in the same category.
  3. Optimize Richa's youtube channel & social media profile to achieve their marketing goals.
